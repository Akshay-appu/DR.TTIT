Academic Advisory Committee

The Academic Advisory Committee (AAC) at Dr. T.T. Institute of Technology (Dr. TTIT) plays a key role in guiding academic policies, curriculum development, and enhancing educational standards. It ensures alignment with industry trends and accreditation requirements, fostering an environment of continuous academic improvement. The AAC comprises experienced faculty members, industry professionals, and administrative staff, working together to offer strategic advice on academic affairs. Their recommendations aim to enrich student learning experiences and promote institutional growth.

Grievance Redressal Committee

The Grievance Redressal Committee at Dr. T.T. Institute of Technology (Dr. TTIT) addresses student and staff concerns in a fair and timely manner. Institution Innovation Council

The Institution Innovation Council (IIC) at Dr. T.T. Institute of Technology (Dr. TTIT) fosters a culture of innovation and entrepreneurship among students.
