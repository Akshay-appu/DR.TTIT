About Us

DR. T. THIMMAIAH INSTITUTE OF TECHNOLOGY

Dr. T. Thimmaiah Institute of Technology (Formerly Known as Golden Valley Institute of Technology - GVIT) was started in the year 1986 at Kolar Gold Fields by Dr. T. Thimmaiah, Ph.D (London), IAS (Retired),
as the founder President under the aegis of Golden Valley Educational Trust, with the sole purpose of imparting quality technical education.
Dr. T. Thimmaiah Institute of Technology is affiliated to Visvesvaraya Technological University (VTU) - Belagavi,
Karnataka and is approved by the All India Council for Technical Education (AICTE) - New Delhi,
and at present offers Undergraduate Programs in Engineering.

ACADEMICS

Dr. TTIT College offers a wide range of academic programs with a focus on quality education and practical learning. The institution is known for its well-structured curriculum, experienced faculty, and state-of-the-art facilities. The college emphasizes innovation, research, and skill development, preparing students for successful careers in various fields like engineering, technology, and management. It encourages a holistic approach to learning, fostering both academic excellence and personal growth.

Library

The College library was established in the year 1986. Initially the library started with 500 books. Today, the institute has a separate library block with an area of 1260 m2 with over 44,405 volumes of books covering all branches of Engineering for reference and issue.

Alumni Association

Dr.TTIT Alumni Association, which is now registered under Karnataka Societies Act 1960, was established to create a network by bridging the gap between budding Dr.TTITians and the accomplished Alumni (GVITians/Dr.TTITians). Our Alumni has made us proud by achieving laurels around the Globe and we wish to showcase the same. Every Graduate of the College is encouraged to become a member of the Alumni Association. The members of the association are actively involved in college development activities by frequent interaction in the course of each academic year.
