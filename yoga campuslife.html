Intranet / Wifi

The entire campus is optically connected to Firewall between buildings and cable within the buildings for connectivity in addition.
Wi-Fi facility is provided in all the buildings.
Dedicated bandwidth of 100 Mbps is taken from Reliance –JIO Communication and 300 Mbps of broadband from Skynet services.
In addition, each building /department of importance like examination center, placement cell, Laboratories and library have dedicated bandwidth of 10+ Mbps.
Internet facility is available for all systems in the whole campus.
All the departments are interconnected through LAN in Wi-Fi environment.
Students are permitted to access the Library computers from 9:00 am to 4.30 pm and students at Hostels are permitted to access the internet from 6:00 pm to 11:00 pm.
The campus LAN is inter-connected to about 540 computers to serve the desktop needs of the students, faculty, and the administrative staff of the College

Hostels
Overview

Providing a homely atmosphere and a suitable ambience to study, separate hostels are built for both Boys (juniors / seniors) & Girls in calm and serene surroundings for students coming from outside places. Over the years the college has built up a reputation as one of the best institutes in providing quality technical education. It’s no wonder the students from all over India seek admission to our college.
All these hostels are located away from each other with separate mess facilities to the need of all the students by providing both North and South Indian Vegetarian & Non - Vegetarian food. Utmost care is taken to maintain hygiene during preparation and serving by dedicated staff. Also separate wardens who are devoted to their task and to look after the welfare of the students with care and dedication.
There are Three Hostels for Boys accommodating 150 students and One Hostel for Girls accommodating 50 Students in twin sharing rooms. Optional washing services are provided in well maintained separate washing areas. A spacious Visitors' Lounge has recently been added for the benefit of visiting parents/guardians, supplementing the residential guest house facilities available. The spotlessly maintained and spacious dining halls in the hostels serve wholesome, nutritious food with the help of a unique steam cooking facility.
